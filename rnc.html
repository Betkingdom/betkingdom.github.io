Data Removal Request

To delete your personal data from Betkingdom, please follow these steps:

  1. Enter the email address associated with your account in the field below.
  2. Click the "Submit" button to initiate the data removal process.
  3. We will send a confirmation email to verify your request.
  4. Once confirmed, your data will be permanently deleted from our system.

Please note that your betting history and account-related information will be deleted, but some anonymized data may be retained for analytical purposes.
